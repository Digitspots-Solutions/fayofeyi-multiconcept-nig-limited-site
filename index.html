About Us

Fayofeyi Multiconcept Nig Limited is a leading provider of quality building materials and financial services in Egbeda, Ibadan, Oyo State. We specialize in supplying premium cement, durable concrete blocks, drainage rings, and offer convenient POS services for seamless transactions. Our commitment is to provide construction professionals, homeowners, and businesses with reliable products at competitive prices. With our strategic location at Fasade, Egbeda, we serve the entire Ibadan metropolis and surrounding areas. We understand the Nigerian construction landscape and are dedicated to supporting your building projects from foundation to finish, while also making payments easier with our POS services.
